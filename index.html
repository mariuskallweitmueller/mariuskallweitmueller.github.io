marius.09mueller(at)outlook(dot)de Marius Müller (*2000, Karlsruhe, DE) is a writer and artist situated in Würzburg, GER. His work both practical and theoretical follows themes of remembrance, articifical neural networks, history and archival techniques. Currently working in a research institute for user interface and digital media practices, he aims to continue his studies of technology and memory in a postgraduate programme.
